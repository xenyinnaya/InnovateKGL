About The Hackathon

Think Big. Build the Future.

A creative innovation challenge designed for students who want to change the world.

Participants will work individually or in teams to develop startup ideas that address real challenges. No coding or business experience is required—just curiosity, creativity, and a willingness to learn.

The Process

How It Works

Four simple steps to launch your startup idea

01

Register Online

Sign up individually or with friends. Create your account and join the innovation community.

02

Kickoff Session

Learn the rules, themes, and judging criteria. Get inspired and meet your mentors.

03

Idea Development

Brainstorm, research, and build your startup idea with mentor support and resources.

04

Final Pitch

Present your idea online to judges and win awards. Showcase your innovation to the world.
